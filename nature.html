Nature in Medellín

Medellín is surrounded by lush green hills and breathtaking natural escapes. Whether you’re hiking, visiting nearby pueblos, or relaxing in botanical gardens, nature is never far away.

Day Trips

From Guatapé’s colorful town and El Peñol’s iconic climb to Pueblito Paisa’s hilltop charm, there’s a world of nature just outside the city.

City Escapes

Inside Medellín, enjoy peaceful walks through the Botanical Garden or explore Arví Park’s forests and trails via the metrocable.
